# Deep Research → Save to docs/research/

Whenever you perform deep research (using the deep-research subagent or web search to investigate a topic), **always save the results as a markdown file in `docs/research/`**.

## Naming convention

Use kebab-case based on the topic:
- `docs/research/gpt-5-2.md`
- `docs/research/langchain4j-and-observability.md`
- `docs/research/discord-java.md`

## What to include

- Date researched at the top
- Primary sources linked
- Key findings summarised
- Any open questions or caveats noted

Do not just present research in the chat and move on — persist it to `docs/research/` so it's available for future reference.
